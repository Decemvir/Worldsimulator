Detailed Description

Member of pattern ChainOfresponsibility as ConcreteHandler. Member of pattern Adapter as Adaptee. An instance of this class represents a high developed life form. This kind of life form is capable of all skills of a middle developed life form. Additional to this it is able to solve problems by thinking a new meme.
