Human * OldestPartnerFinderStrategy::searchPartnersAndCreateHuman ( void  )
virtual

Searches the HumanLifeChain for the oldest Woman and Man. It finds partners with similar age. It calls createHuman() of Woman giving her the Man. That creates an Human (Woman or Man randomly). The Human is put into the HumanLifeChain and returned. If no partners are found 0 is returned..
