Detailed Description

Member of pattern ChainOfresponsibility as ConcreteHandler. Member of pattern Adapter as Adaptee. An instance of this class represents a low developed life form. This kind of life form has a 4 possibilities to react on problems: it may know or the solution, ignore the problem or ask a known life from, which is higher developed.
