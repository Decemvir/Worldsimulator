Detailed Description

Member of pattern Singleton. Member of pattern Facade as Facade. Member of pattern Command as Caller. Defines a handler that holds a job queue for every existing life form to easily deal with situations like the death/deletion of a life form instance in a centralized manner so they don't have to deal with job specific problems.

The main functionality of this class is to have the ability to replace references in jobs globally or remove jobs with a specific reference.

The basic functionality of adding jobs and working on the top priority job of a life form is also given.
